Full Body Workout

This workout includes exercises for all major muscle groups. It's designed to help you build strength and endurance in a balanced way.

Workout Benefits:

  • Targets all major muscle groups
  • Improves overall strength and endurance
  • Burns fat and increases metabolism
  • Improves posture and body mechanics
  • Can be done at home with minimal equipment

How to Perform Each Exercise:

  • Squats: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart. Lower your body as if sitting back into a chair, keeping your knees behind your toes. Push through your heels to return to standing.
  • Push-ups: Start in a plank position with hands placed slightly wider than shoulder-width. Lower your body until your chest almost touches the floor, then push back up.
  • Plank: Lie face down, then lift your body off the floor, balancing on your toes and forearms. Keep your body in a straight line, holding the position for as long as possible.
  • Lunges: Step forward with one foot and lower your body until both knees are bent at 90-degree angles. Push off the front foot to return to the starting position.
  • Jumping Jacks: Stand with feet together and arms at your sides. Jump your feet out while raising your arms above your head, then return to the starting position.
